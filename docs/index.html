Steve is a college graduate, espresso fanatic, and book lover. He wants to open up a coffee shop that caters to people just like him. He decides to call it Book and Beans. New customers stop in, order a drink of their choice, and then can browse Steve's wonderful library of self help books and historical non fiction. (There just happen to be his two favorite genres.) Business is actually amazing, and Steve can't complain.

But, he's run across one problem. His target drinker tends to be environmentally conscious. This is something Steve completely resonates with. At the same time, he also loves rewarding his regular walk ins. He currently uses a punch card system, but this really isn't the "greenest" solution. One day, he decides to ditch the punch cards all together. Instead of making people burn through tiny pieces of paper to earn a free cup, he wants to take his loyalty rewards mobile. He signs up for an account with Slick Text. He reserves the keyword BEANS, and drinkers interested in receiving weekly deals text this word to 31996. Trees are saved, the loyalty program is more efficient, and as a result; business grows.
